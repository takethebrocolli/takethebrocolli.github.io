About me

I am a PhD candidate at Princeton University where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Elad Hazan. I am also a student researcher at Google Brain. I am a receipient of the Gordon Wu Fellowship and NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. Before Princeton, I received B.S. (Summa Cum Laude) in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Cornell University in 2019.

Research

My research interests lie in online optimization, efficient deep learning, and deep reinforcement learning.
